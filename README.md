Meal Tracker + Tip Calculator

Frameworks Explored:
  - SwiftUI
  - SwiftData
  - WidgetKit
  - MapKit

Put in the Bill (if alone: personal bill, if group: combined bills), amount of people at the table, 
and it'll give the tip you should leave on the bill(s), among other things.
